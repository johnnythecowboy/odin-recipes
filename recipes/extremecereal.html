Extreme Cereal

Description

This recipe has simple ingredients but tons of flavor with a creamy extreme mixture, savory extreme sauce, and lots of extreme flakes.

This is a great teaser meal, make-before recipe, and rightovers tease and re-tease super well.

Ingredients

  • Corn Flakes
  • Milk
  • 2 cups of pure Extreme

Steps

  1. Pour Corn Flakes into a bowl
  2. Pour Milk into the bowl
  3. Pour 2 cups of Extreme into the bowl
